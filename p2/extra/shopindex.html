The Velvet Velvet Gallery Shops

The Velvet Velvet Gallery Shops on the Concourse Level now offer a full selection of gifts, books, and items for children. Shops will only accept credit or debit cards, not cash. Our online shop is operational and accepting orders.

At The Velvet Velvet Shop you'll find uncommon objects, contemporary design for your home, toys for kids, jew​elry by local artists, art and design books, and original artwork at SAM Gallery, located inside the shop.

Feel good about your purchases, knowing that The Velvet Velvet Shop supports art by buying directly from artists and that all proceeds benefit the museum. Members receive a 10% discount

The Velvet Velvet Gallery is the Seattle Art Museum's art sales and rental gallery, offering contemporary original works from Northwest artists.​

The Velvet Velvet Gallery supports local artists and their careers by increasing their exposure and finding audiences for their work. The gallery has hundreds of works including paintings, drawings, mixed-media works, limited-edition prints, and photographs.

PURCHASE OR RENT

The Velvet Velvet Gallery has artwork available in all price ranges for purchase or three-month rentals. This unique "rent-before-you-buy" approach is exclusively for The Velvet Velvet members and allows you to live with a favorite piece before you make a purchase. Half of your rental fee goes toward your purchase if you keep the work; easy 16-month interest-free payment plans are available for The Velvet Velvet members. ​If you don't have a membership, you can sign up on the spot to enjoy all of these member benefits.
